Critical Timeline

Bhadra 19

The Trigger

Government bans social media platforms, citing "unregulated activities." Youth interpret this as an attack on their lifeline.

Bhadra 23

The Outbreak

Maitighar protests breach barricades at Baneshwor. 19 confirmed deaths as police deploy lethal force. Nationwide synchronization begins.

Bhadra 24

State of Chaos

Curfew ignored. Protesters target state symbols (Singha Durbar, Baluwatar). Widespread looting by peripheral groups.

Asoj 05

Investigation

Judicial Commission formed to investigate facts and recommend systemic reforms for a new Nepal.

Systemic Revolutions

The commission's 17-point recommendation focuses on rebuilding the state-citizen contract.

  • 01 Security Reform: Operational autonomy for the IGP and modernization of lethal force protocols.
  • 02 Judicial Oversight: Independent audit of extrajudicial fatalities during the protests.
  • 03 Digital Rights: Repeal of blanket platform bans and shift towards collaborative cyber-governance.
  • 04 Political Reset: Term limits and merit-based leadership in constitutional bodies.
